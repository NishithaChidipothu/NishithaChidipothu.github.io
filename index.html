Bio

Hello, my name is Nishitha Chidipothu. I come from a very small town in India and in pursuit of my dreams I shifted to the US for my Masters. I really enjoy traveling and I must say New York is my favorite city. I have always been passionate about science behind the usage of data and this passion led the way to my Masters.
